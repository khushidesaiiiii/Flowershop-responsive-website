About Us

Garden Fresh

Why choose us?

At Prose Petal, we curate the freshest, hand-picked flowers directly from local growers. Each arrangement is meticulously crafted to bring joy and beauty to your special moments. Our floral artists create unique and captivating designs. From elegant bouquets to enchanting centerpieces, Prose Petal arrangements are a feast for the eyes. We believe in building relationships. Our knowledgeable staff will guide you in selecting the perfect arrangement for any occasion, ensuring a memorable experience. Whether it’s a birthday surprise or an expression of love, Prose Petal ensures prompt and reliable delivery. Your flowers arrive looking vibrant and ready to delight. Prose Petal is more than a flower shop; we’re part of the community. We support local events and charities, spreading the joy of flowers far and wide.

We have successfully provided flowers for many weddings and occasions, small to large.
